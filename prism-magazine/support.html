Support Prism

Prism is a completely non-profit organisation and is keen to get your support. We need good writers, more readers, and financial contributors. If you like the idea of Prism and its vision, please think seriously about supporting us.

If you have ideas and feedback or if you can write good articles, stories or poetry, please read our writing guide and contact us.

For better or worse, to make Prism nice we need money. None of us gets paid, so any donations will cover software, internet costs, and printing. A big thank you to everyone who’s already donated, including all the anonymous doners!

You can pay your subscription or donate by one of these methods:

  • Transfer money directly into our ANZ bank account: Prism magazine, [account now closed]. When you do, be sure to include your name in the reference, and email us when you’re done.
  • Write out a cheque to Prism magazine and post it to our admin person.
  • Use your credit card, via PayPal. There’s a small fee involved in this; just email the editor when you want an invoice emailed to you.
