Prism magazine

Prism was a small-scale printed magazine that ran from Aug 2005 to Jan 2007, and was put together by a bunch of Kiwis with a big-hearted Christian vision and a dose of enthusiasm.

We stopped publishing when the editor moved from merely fathering a magazine to being a real father (and helping start a company).

It was a worthwhile journey for us Prismatics. We’d still be interested to hear your thoughts on any of the articles – the back issues will stay online for the foreseeable. Just for the record, we’ve left most of the other website content as is.

We also add the odd piece of writing to our articles page as things come up.
